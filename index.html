About me

I am Bom Bahadur Thapa, a recent BEng (Hons) Software Engineering graduate from the University of Greenwich, London. My journey in software engineering has been both challenging and rewarding, equipping me with a robust skill set in Python, JavaScript, Java, and web development. Throughout my studies and professional experiences, such as my mentorship with Oracle and internship at HUB IT Training & Solution in Nepal, I have honed my abilities in project leadership and Agile methodologies.

I take pride in my academic achievements, particularly earning first-class honours, and the successful completion of various projects. Notably, I developed a Python-based NLP framework for text summarization and keyword extraction, and led teams in creating innovative applications like a multi-threaded GUI chat app and a London Underground route planner. Participating in hackathons has been exhilarating, especially winning first place in AC-HACK 2022 for our mental health support website. Bilingual in English and Nepali, I excel in leadership, adaptability, and motivating others. My passion for software engineering drives me to continuously improve and contribute effectively to any team or project I am part of.
